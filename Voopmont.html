Voopmont Wiki

Voopmont, officially the District of Voomont, is a district in north-western SpookVooper. At approximately 10 million square kilometers, Voopmont is the largest district in the Vooperian Empire. Voopmont is also the northernmost district in the Vooperian Empire. Voopmont is bordered to the south by Landing Cove, south-west by New Spudland District, and east by New Yam District. The district's territory is composed of the mainland and Icelesia, an island off the north coast. Voopmont has a population of 250 million. The capital city of Voomont is Voopelier. Voopelier is also the most populous city in Voomont.
